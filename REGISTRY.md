## Medium Description (Smithery/Glama/mcp.run)

Query 5,775 Belgian statutes — from the Loi sur la protection des donnees personnelles and
Wet betreffende de informatiebeveiliging to the Code judiciaire — directly from Claude,
Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client. Full-text search across 142,743 provisions with
EU cross-references. Bilingual French/Dutch.

Built by Ansvar Systems (ansvar.eu) — part of Ansvar Open Law, providing free structured
access to legislation from 70+ jurisdictions worldwide.

This is a research tool, not legal advice. Verify critical citations against official sources
(Belgisch Staatsblad / Moniteur belge).
